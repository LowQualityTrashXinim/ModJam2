Hidden underground, a portal to a ancient, cursed kingdom ... Are you brave enough to enter ?

Cursed Kingdom is a mod that added in a subworld that hidden in deep caven, you can naturally come across it in pre Hardmode,
you can also get access to it via getting a drop from Wall of flesh, inside Cursed Kingdom domain you can't naturally regenerate and
dangerous enemy await for you but in return the loot in the cursed kingdom worth the risk.

This mod is made for a mod jam 2, hope you guys enjoy
